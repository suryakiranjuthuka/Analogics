Welcome to Analogics Group

The journey of Analogics Tech India Ltd. began in 1994, as the first electronic manufacturing company to supply Hand Held Terminals for Spot Billing and Collection solution to the Power Utility Sector in India. Since our inception, the focus has been on understanding customer needs and providing innovative technological products, thus making us a market leader in Hand Held portable devices. The integrated manufacturing approach – from design to delivery, is what our customers’ value the most and has been one of the drivers of our success.

We believe in being at the forefront of technological advancement and it is this belief that led us to constantly innovate and provide everything ranging from hardware and software to services. We have constantly adapted to the global trends changing needs of our customers by transitioning from a Hardware & IT product company to a company that can provide end-to-end solutions in various sectors.

Our commitment towards meeting evolving customer needs and achieving utmost satisfaction, has led to the development of new products and solutions over the past two decades. Today, our portfolio of products and solutions cater to various needs in multiple sectors including Utilities, Transportation, Banking, Solar, Defence, Police and Security. We also provide products and solutions for Time & Attendance requirement, Spot Billing & Ticketing demands and Retail Billing applications.

To date we have worked with over 100 clients around the world and delivered 650K+ Hand Held Computers. All this has been possible with our efficient workforce of over 500 employees spread across India and a strong global network of partners.

Integrity, Excellence and Innovation have been our core values guiding every business decision since foundation and will continue to do so. Our motivation to set a benchmark in creating efficient operations in numerous domains will drive our success in the future.

Leadership Management

Mr. Surender Reddy

Founder and Managing Director

"From our inception, Mr Surender Reddy envisioned and worked towards making Analogics a technology-focussed and solution-oriented organization."

An Engineering Post Graduate, M Tech. in Advanced Electronics from JNT University, Hyderabad, he has started his career as an Engineer in the year 1978 with ISRO in the field of Design and Development of Space Systems at SAC, Ahmedabad. Subsequently he worked for 16 years in National Remote Sensing Agency, Department of Space, Hyderabad in the field of Design and Development of Remote Sensing Systems for Mineral Exploration and Mapping applications. He resigned from his services from NRSA (now NRSC) and joined in this Company as Executive Director. He has about 40 years of experience in Electronic industry and the success of the firm has direct bearing to the hard work and dedication put in by the Promoter.

Mr. B Maruthy

Director, Research and Development

"Under his supervision, the company has created innovative products and solutions for energy and utilities segments"

An M. Tech in Advanced Electronics from JNT University, Hyderabad, he has started his career as an Engineer Assistant in the year 1981 with BHEL (R&D). Subsequently he worked for 22 years in National Remote Sensing Agency, Department of Space, Hyderabad, in the field of Data Acquisition Aerial (DAA). He resigned from his services from NRSA (now NRSC) and joined in this Company as a Director. He has more than 30 years of experience in Electronic Industry and has been leading and guiding the R&D and Production teams of the company.

Mr. D Prashanth

Director

Mr. Prashanth is the youngest Director of Analogics and has been associated with the company since 2003. He specialized in Bachelors of Technology – Electronics & Communications from Osmania University in the year 2001 and thereafter worked for 2 years as a Trainee Engineer with Research Centre Imarat (RCI), Hyderabad.
